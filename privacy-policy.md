# Privacy Policy for Meetly

**Last updated: 2026-06-02**

Meetly ("we", "us") is a social-networking app that helps people meet new friends, find events, and have decent conversations. We respect your privacy and built this app to collect as little data as possible.

## 1. What we collect

When you sign up, we collect:
- Your **name** (the one you choose to show others)
- Your **phone number or email** (for sign-in only)
- Your **date of birth** (to confirm you're 18+)
- Your **gender** (if you choose to provide it)

When you use the app, we collect (only what you give us):
- **Profile photos** you upload
- **Bio, occupation, city** that you fill in
- **Interests, hobbies, what you're looking for** (selected from our lists)
- **Social handles** (Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.) — only if you add them
- **Approximate location** (only if you grant location permission) — used to show people nearby. We don't store location history.
- **Events you host or attend, friendships, messages, RSVPs**

We do not collect:
- Your contact list
- Anything from outside the app
- Anything not listed above

## 2. How we store it

All data is stored on **Supabase** (a SOC2-compliant cloud database provider in the EU). Photos are stored in Supabase Storage.

## 3. Who we share it with

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal data to any third party. Other Meetly users only see what you choose to put on your public profile.

The only third parties that process your data on our behalf are:
- **Supabase** — to store your data and authenticate your sign-in
- **Twilio** — to send SMS / WhatsApp verification codes (only if you sign in by phone)

## 4. How long we keep it

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account (Settings → Delete account), we permanently remove your profile, messages, hosted events, and photos within 30 days.

## 5. Your rights

You can at any time:
- **View** all your data via the app
- **Edit** any of your data via Edit Profile
- **Delete** your account permanently from Settings
- **Block or report** any user who's behaving badly
- **Request a copy** of your data — email us (see contact below)

If you're in the EU/UK, you also have rights under GDPR including the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

## 6. Cookies and tracking

We do not use cookies, trackers, or third-party analytics SDKs.

## 7. Children

Meetly is for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has signed up, please report them in the app or email us.

## 8. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and Row Level Security on all database tables. No system is perfectly secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Changes to this policy

If we materially change this policy, we will notify you in the app. Continued use of the app after the change means you accept the updated policy.

## 10. Contact

Questions, requests, or complaints? Email us at: **email.cslondon@gmail.com**

We aim to respond within 5 business days.
