Common questions

How do I delete my account?

Open Mingly → tap Me tab → tap the ⚙ gear icon → scroll to Danger zone → tap Delete account. Your profile, messages, hosted events, and photos are permanently removed within 30 days.

Someone is harassing me. What do I do?

  • Open their profile → tap ⚠ Report this person at the bottom → pick a reason → submit. We review every report within 24h.
  • Tick the "Also block this person" box so they can't contact you any more.
  • You can manage your blocked users in Settings → Privacy & safety.

I'm not getting verification codes via SMS / WhatsApp

  • Make sure you included the country code (e.g. +44 for UK, +1 for US)
  • Wait 60 seconds and tap Resend code
  • If still nothing, email us with your number — sometimes carriers block automated SMS

Can I change my phone number?

Not yet from the app — email us with your current and new numbers.

How do I host an event?

Open the Events tab → tap + Host top right → fill in title, category, location (with autocomplete), date/time, optional cover image, optional price (Professional events only) → tap Create event.

Why can't I message someone?

You can only message people who have accepted your friend request. Visit their profile → tap Add as friend → wait for them to accept. Then "Say hi" appears as an option.

Will Mingly always be free?

The core app is free. Some hosts may charge for professional events (e.g. workshops, dinners). You'll always see the price clearly before joining.

Where is my data stored?

On Supabase servers (EU region). See our Privacy Policy for details.

Bug reports

When emailing about a bug, please include:

  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened instead
  • iPhone model + iOS version (Settings → General → About)
  • Mingly version (Me → Settings → About → App version)
  • Screenshots if possible
